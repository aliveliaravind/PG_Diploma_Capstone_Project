How it works

Enter a username to get personalized product recommendations ranked by actual customer sentiment from reviews.

1

Enter Username

Type or select a username, then click "Get Recommendations".

2

Get 20 Product Matches

The system finds the top 20 products that user is most likely to purchase based on similar users' ratings using User-Based Collaborative Filtering.

3

Analyze Review Sentiment

We scan customer reviews for those 20 products to determine which ones receive the most positive feedback using a Logistic Regression sentiment model.

4

Deliver Top 5 Picks

Finally, we rank and show you the top 5 best recommendations based on each product's positive review sentiment rate.
